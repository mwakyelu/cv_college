We Provide Opportunities to Become

WHY INVEST AT CV COLLEGE EDUCATION AID?

Pending the standard and caliber of teachers and of the environment we nurture our students, majority of our graduates will either join working team or go to university. Their mean average salary will range from 450,000 to 1,600,000 depending on the program undertaken. That says a lot about the return on the investment at CV College education.

But facts and figures don’t tell the whole story. CV College students graduate with the experience of living and learning in vibrant, tropical metropolitan city around Africa.

They don’t just attend lectures by achievers in their fields. They create, perform, and conduct pragmatic field works alongside them.

They forge friendships with peers who seem completely different, but in fact share passions like exploring every detail of an artist, inventor, song, meal, town, or topic.

FUNDIND OPTIONS

To pay for their education, most students and families combine funding types such as scholarships, grants, loans, and savings. CVC’s Office of Financial Aid can help you coordinate these various options.

We encourage you — no matter your financial situation — to apply for financial aid as soon as possible. Meeting our deadlines will help you maximize your financial aid options.

Of note is the reality that, grants are very few and vary in number every year pending availability from donors. Therefore meticulous scrutiny process is embedded with selection of true needy students to be offered meagerly available grants.
