HELPPER Program

Healthcare Leveraging via Pathologist and Physician Encounters in Remote areas – Program (HELPPER Program)

HELPPER-Program

The moving specialized hospital’ is planned to run as a not-for-profit, comprehensive mobile healthcare polyclinic that travels to sidelined rural Tanzania to equalize access to basic health services. The first phase of this program focuses on Women, Girls, and Children by prioritizing Screening and diagnosis of Breast cancer, Cervical cancer, and Pediatric cancers" alongside with diabetes and hypertension screening which costs a glucometer and blood pressure machine respectively.

INVASION

Invented by Dr. Salvatory Makweta Mlaga, HELPPER Program write-up has been announced as the winner by the WHO in NCD Lab First cycle 2021 (visit WHO knowledge action portal on NCD). One of the 11 winners globally.

HELPPER Mission

The Mission of this program is to leverage healthcare provision system by deploying general and specialist medical care providers to do early detection, timely diagnosis, and appropriate treatment guidance at the vicinity of the marginalized remote users. This means we take quality services close to the home of those who could not otherwise reach it, those if not followed would never afford to travel and meet a specialist doctor during their whole life.

Our Services

Preventive and Diagnostic Services

HELPPER Program staff members provide screenings for cervical cancer, breast cancer, blood pressure, cholesterol, blood glucose (blood sugar), obesity (Weight/ Body Mass Index, as well as pregnancy testing, and HIV counseling and testing. With no required insurance and at no cost, we provide a variety of services that help to keep our clients healthy and prevent serious diseases and illnesses.

We do a peculiar on-site pathology screening and diagnosis services with pap smears and/ or fine needle aspiration cytology by physical or ultrasound guidance. With these secondary preventive measures we facilitate early detection and diagnosis of life threatening conditions like cancers for timely treatment plans, hence save lives.

Treatment and Treatment Referral

We provide onsite treatment to most common minor ailments, while providing treatment referral linkages to nearby specialized centers for cancer care and other health facilities for treatment of diabetes, hypertension and other disorders.
