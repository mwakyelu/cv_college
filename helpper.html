HELPPER Program

Healthcare Leveraging via Pathologist and Physician Encounters in Remote areas – Program (HELPPER Program)

Our Vision

To become the superior and dependable provider of solutions to accurate and affordable high-quality healthcare services to marginalized communities within their localities.

Mission Statement

To leverage healthcare provision system by deploying general and specialist medical care providers to do early detection, timely diagnosis and appropriate treatment guidance at the vicinity of the marginalized remote users

HELPPER-Program

The moving specialized hospital’ is planned to run as a not-for-profit, comprehensive mobile healthcare polyclinic that travels to sidelined rural Tanzania to equalize access to basic health services. The first phase of this program focuses on Women, Girls, and Children by prioritizing Screening and diagnosis of Breast cancer, Cervical cancer, and Pediatric cancers" alongside with diabetes and hypertension screening which costs a glucometer and blood pressure machine respectively.

HELPPER-Program

Invented by Dr. Salvatory Makweta Mlaga, HELPPER Program write-up has been announced as the winner by the WHO in NCD Lab First cycle 2021 (visit WHO knowledge action portal on NCD, web link https://www.knowledge-action-portal.com/en/ncd_lab/featured_projects). One of the 11 winners globally.

HELPPER-Program

The Mission of this program is to leverage healthcare provision system by deploying general and specialist medical care providers to do early detection, timely diagnosis, and appropriate treatment guidance at the vicinity of the marginalized remote users. This means we take quality services close to the home of those who could not otherwise reach it, those if not followed would never afford to travel and meet a specialist doctor during their whole life.
