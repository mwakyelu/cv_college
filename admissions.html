FUNDIND OPTIONS

To pay for their education, most students and families combine funding types such as scholarships, grants, loans, and savings. CVC’s Office of Financial Aid can help you coordinate these various options.

We encourage you — no matter your financial situation — to apply for financial aid as soon as possible. Meeting our deadlines will help you maximize your financial aid options.

Of note is the reality that, grants are very few and vary in number every year pending availability from donors. Therefore meticulous scrutiny process is embedded with selection of true needy students to be offered meagerly available grants.

For more Info, visit the Financial Aid Page

There will be two regular cycles of student enrollments according to NACTE arrangements October and March intakes.

Applications for October intakes usually opens in July each year and closes in early August. For this intake college selection and allocations a published in September and commences studies in October each year.

While for March intake applications do start towards the end of the year before study year. In 2021 for istance it will open in November 2021and Studies will commence in March 2022.

All intakes leads to the same weight of programs. You are strongly advised to make use of these windows.

Prospective students can apply to the college directly via online system and will be directed to confirm application with NACTE later in the process.

Short Courses at CVC

In view of skills gap in various service sectors in Tanzania CV College team has improvised solution based shortcourses. The following are employment guaranteed short courses provided at CV College from October 2021.

  • Certificate in Multipurpose Cleaning
  • Fumigation Technician Certificate
  • Landscaping and Gardening Certificate
  • Beginners and Business English Courses

Other professional oriented short courses will be announced in the near future.


Duration and Timeframes:

All short courses will be run for six months. There will be regular track intake which invites students who have time space for morning schedules starting from 8am to 12:30pm. Also the college has an evening class program for all short courses starting from 2pm to 06pm.
